The JMR iFarms Story

Revolutionizing Your Plate, One Microgreen at a Time

The Problem with Freshness

We believe everyone deserves easy access to the freshest, most nutrient-dense food possible. Yet, traditional gardening requires time, space, and a certain expertise that many of us simply don't have in our busy urban lives. The journey from a distant farm to your plate often means lost nutrients and questionable pesticides.

We asked: What if you could grow pure, healthy food right in your home space with zero fuss?

The Seed of an Idea: Smarter Farming

Our journey began and was driven by a simple mission: to make healthy, home-grown produce accessible to everyone. We decided to tackle the challenges of gardening using intelligent technology. The result? A compact, elegant automated system that eliminates the need for messy soil, constant attention, and a green thumb.

Engineering Effortless Nutrition

We spent years researching, prototyping, and perfecting our technology. This wasn't just about growing plants; it was about engineering the perfect environment.

Your Farm, Your Home

The final product is the JMR iFarms automated Microgreens grow unit – a sleek, silent, and simple-to-use appliance at home. It’s a testament to our dedication to quality and sustainability.
